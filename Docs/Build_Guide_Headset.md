# FPV AI Headset Build Guide

## Bill of Materials (BOM)
* ESP32-S3 Sense (XIAO)
* 0.42-inch I2C OLED Display
* 102050 LiPo Battery & TP4056 Charger
* 5V Boost Converter & 1A Slide Switch
* 2x Magnifier (Combiner Lens)

## 3D Printing Parameters
* **Material:** TPU (Battery Sleeve), PETG/PLA (Optics Mount)
* **Infill:** 20%
* **Supports:** Needed for the glasses clip overhangs.

## Assembly Instructions
1. **Power Pipeline:** Wire the battery to the TP4056, through the switch, to the boost converter. 
2. **Optics:** Mount the OLED exactly 50mm from the camera lens.
3. **Combiner:** Slot the 2x magnifier at a 45-degree angle.
